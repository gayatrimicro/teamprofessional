Waterjet Technology

You are here: New Ideas & Technologies - Waterjet Technology

At Team Professional, our waterjet cutting services are a cut above the rest. Our machinery and programming software help us to create flawless designs out of raw material. By staying up-to-date in the industry, and using cutting edge waterjet technology, we give our customers quality parts and products along with competitive pricing.

There are many applications where abrasive waterjet cutting technology is the cutting method of choice. Abrasive waterjet is an excellent choice when cutting thick materials, whereas laser cutting is ideal for thin steels and glass. Waterjet cutting is also great for reflective materials that laser shouldn't or can't cut including; glass, copper, brass, titanium, wood, acrylic, plastic, and more. You can compare laser cutting and waterjet cutting if you would like to see more of the differences.

Team Professional features ultra-high pressure waterjet cutting and machining with fast turnaround on parts. Our waterjet cutting services use water compressed up to 60,000 PSI as a powerful and precise cutting tool in manufacturing. The jet stream generated by waterjet cutting is capable of cutting material from 1mm thick up to 60 mm thick.

Benefits of Waterjet

  • Improve part tolerance and geometry
  • Cutting of parts up to 400% faster than conventional flat stock waterjet cutting machines
  • Virtually eliminate taper
  • Reduce part cost
  • True part stacking ability with consistent tolerance layer to layer
  • No secondary finishing required
  • Unmatched versatility; cut any shape, out of any material, of any thickness
  • Cutting of parts using virtually any CAD file or scan drawings
