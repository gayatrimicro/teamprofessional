Vision & Mission

You are here: Home - About Us

We want positive change

  • We have a positive attitude towards change and we are quick to implement.
  • We are honest and fair team players guided by entrepreneurial spirit, mutual respect and a desire to keep things simple.
  • We measure our performance in order to learn and to improve continuously.

We seek partnership

  • We are committed to providing maximum customer value through innovation and quality. We want to be our customers' preferred partner.
  • Better performance and service unites us with customers and suppliers.
  • In those selected market segments we concentrate on, we target a leadership position measured on a global scale.

We need commitment

  • Our people are fully committed to fulfilling our customers' needs.
  • They are loyal and highly qualified. They promote continues improvement.
  • As co-entrepreneurs, they assume responsibility and claim the necessary authority. Their career development, compensation and rewards are based on performance.

We seek success

  • Through long-term above average development, Team Professional maintains its independence. We achieve this through positive dynamics, adequate earnings and international orientation.
  • Success makes us attractive and reliable to customers, suppliers, people and investors.

We respect the community

  • We apply ourselves actively for the community and the environment. We promote free enterprise and free-market economy and democracy.
  • Team Professional products, services, factories and jobs should be models of innovation, safety and efficiency.
