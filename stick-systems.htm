Conventional Curtain wall & stick systems

You are here: New Ideas & Technologies - Conventional Curtain wall & stick systems

Team Professional offers the full range of system and custom-designed windows and curtain walling incorporating full thermal breaks, drainage and the rain screen - principle of pressure equalization. Finishing of aluminum is available in anodizing and polyester powder coating.

Unitized Curtain Wall Systems Custom System Design:

  • Fully unitized, pre-glazed, and stick type curtain wall system.
  • Metal and stone architectural claddings.
  • Point fixed structural glazing.

Drawing Preparation:

  • Tender drawings
  • Shop drawings
  • Fabrication drawings
  • Aluminum and gasket extrusion die drawings
  • Three-dimensional studies of facade areas or connection details.

Structural Analysis:

  • Detailed structural analysis to accompany wall system Automated computation and report generation for moist calculations
  • Finite element modeling of plates and solid elements (such as glass panes and cast brackets). Both negligible-displacement algorithms, and iterative techniques for significant display conditions are offered.
  • Analysis of dynamic pressure responses within ventilated ca determines appropriate design pressure reductions.
  • Using a finite element analysis with criteria based on ASTM to analysis the glass, which could be subjected to human impact loading.
  • Calculating the total U factor for the curtain wall based on the external and internal temperatures.
